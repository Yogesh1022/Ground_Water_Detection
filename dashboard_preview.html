THE CRISIS

Deccan Basalt Aquifer Running Dry

Vidarbha sits on Deccan basalt — a hard rock aquifer with limited storage. Monsoon recharge lasts only 3 months, but pumping happens year-round.

🌾   Top Soil (0–0.5m) — Black Cotton / Red Laterite
🪨   Regolith (0.5–5m) — Weathered zone
🔩   Weathered Basalt (5–25m) — Shallow wells tap here
⬛   Fractured Basalt (25–80m) — Primary aquifer
💧   Deep Aquifer (80–300m) — Crisis drilling zone
